Drop your IQ Noodles screenshots in this folder to fill the case-study page.

Expected filenames (used by /internship):
  scan.jpg       — pointing the camera at the puzzle board
  detected.jpg   — the app recognising the pieces
  solution.jpg   — the solution shown in 3D over the board
  validate.jpg   — checking whether placements are correct

Tips:
- Phone screenshots (portrait, ~9:16) look best — the slots are portrait.
- JPG or PNG both work; if you use PNG, change the extensions in
  components/internship/InternshipContent.tsx accordingly.
- Until a file exists, the page shows a tidy placeholder, so it always
  looks complete.
